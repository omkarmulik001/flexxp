  • Step 1: Set the cable machine to chest height, using D-handles.
  • Step 2: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, facing the machine.
  • Step 3: Grasp the handles with an overhand grip, maintaining a slight bend in the elbows.
  • Step 4: Extend your arms laterally, squeezing your shoulder blades together.
  • Step 5: Control the return to the starting position.

Standing Cable Reverse Fly Overview

Standing Cable Reverse Fly isolates the rear deltoids and upper back muscles, delivering a targeted exercise for sculpting definition. Using a cable machine with D-handles, this movement emphasizes controlled lateral lifts, enhancing strength in the posterior shoulder region.

Benefits of The Standing Cable Reverse Fly

This exercise isolates and enhances the rear deltoids and upper back, contributing to a well-defined and sculpted appearance. The cable resistance ensures constant tension, optimizing muscle engagement and promoting controlled lateral movements. Standing Cable Reverse Fly is particularly effective for those seeking to improve posture and develop a strong upper back.
