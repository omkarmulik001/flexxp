Grilled Chicken with Roasted Vegetables

Grilled chicken with roasted vegetables is a delectable dish that combines the succulence of perfectly grilled chicken with the earthy flavors of assorted vegetables. Packed with nutrients and bursting with flavors, this recipe is not only easy to make but also a wholesome meal for any occasion. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the ingredients, step-by-step preparation, nutritional information, and a few additional tips to make this dish a staple in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Facts

Calories 350
Protein 30g
Fat 18g
Carbohydrates 14g
Fiber 6g

Cooking Steps

    Step 1: Marinate the Chicken
  • In a bowl, combine olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, salt, pepper, and lemon juice
  • Coat the chicken breasts with the marinade, ensuring even coverage
    • Step 2: Preheat the Grill
  • Preheat your grill to medium-high heat
    • Step 3: Grill the Chicken
  • Grill for 6-8 minutes per side or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C)
    • Step 4: Prepare the Roasted Vegetables
  • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C)
  • In a large baking dish, combine cherry tomatoes, sliced bell peppers, zucchini, and red onion
  • Drizzle olive oil over the vegetables, sprinkle with dried rosemary, salt, and pepper
  • Roast in the oven for 20-25 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and slightly caramelized
    • Step 5: Assemble the Dish
  • Arrange sliced grilled chicken on a serving platter
  • Garnish with chopped fresh parsley
